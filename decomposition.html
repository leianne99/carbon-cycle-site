🌱 Decomposition

Decomposition is the process of breaking down dead plants, animals, and organic matter into simpler substances.

🦠 Who Does It?

  • Bacteria – tiny microbes that break down soft tissues
  • Fungi – such as mushrooms, help recycle nutrients
  • Invertebrates – like worms, insects, and beetles also assist

🧬 What Happens During Decomposition?

  • Carbon stored in dead organisms is released into the soil and air as CO₂ or methane
  • Nutrients like nitrogen and phosphorus are returned to the ecosystem
  • This process enriches the soil and helps new plants grow

🌍 Why It Matters:

  • Recycles carbon and other nutrients back into the ecosystem
  • Prevents dead matter from piling up
  • Supports soil health and plant growth
