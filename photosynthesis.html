☀️ Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is a chemical process used by plants, algae, and some bacteria to turn sunlight, carbon dioxide (CO₂), and water into food (glucose) and oxygen.

🌿 How It Works:

  • Plants absorb CO₂ from the air through tiny openings called stomata.
  • Roots pull in water from the soil.
  • Chlorophyll (the green pigment in leaves) captures sunlight.
  • The plant uses sunlight energy to convert water and CO₂ into glucose (sugar) and oxygen.

🧪 The Formula:

6CO₂ + 6H₂O + sunlight → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂
(Carbon dioxide + Water + Sunlight → Glucose + Oxygen)

🌍 Why It Matters:

  • Removes CO₂ from the atmosphere (helps fight climate change)
  • Produces oxygen for humans and animals to breathe
  • Creates food at the base of the food chain
