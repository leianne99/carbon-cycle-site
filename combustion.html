🔥 Combustion

Combustion is the process of burning substances like fossil fuels (coal, oil, natural gas) or wood. This releases stored carbon into the atmosphere as carbon dioxide (CO₂).

🚗 Examples of Combustion:

  • Cars burning gasoline
  • Factories burning coal or oil
  • Wildfires burning trees and grass

🧪 The Formula (for burning fossil fuels):

Fuel + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O + heat
(Fossil fuel + Oxygen → Carbon dioxide + Water + Energy)

⚠️ Why It’s a Problem:

  • Releases large amounts of CO₂ quickly
  • Overloads the natural carbon cycle
  • Major contributor to climate change and global warming

💡 Did You Know?

The carbon in fossil fuels comes from ancient plants and animals buried millions of years ago!
