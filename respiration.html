🐾 Respiration

Respiration is the process by which animals (including humans) and plants break down food molecules (like glucose) to release energy.

🐛 What Happens During Respiration:

  • Organisms eat plants or other animals to get food (glucose).
  • Inside their cells, glucose reacts with oxygen.
  • This reaction produces energy, water, and carbon dioxide (CO₂).

🧪 The Formula:

C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + Energy
(Glucose + Oxygen → Carbon dioxide + Water + Energy)

🌬️ Why It Matters:

  • Releases CO₂ back into the atmosphere (keeps the carbon cycle going)
  • Supplies energy for movement, growth, and survival
  • Occurs in all living things — plants do it too!
